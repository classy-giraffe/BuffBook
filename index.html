BuffBook

A practical, evidence-based guide to hypertrophy programming with a minimal-volume, maximum-efficiency philosophy.

BuffBook is a practical training manual for lifters who want better hypertrophy outcomes with less wasted volume. The chapters blend evidence and actionable programming so you can train hard, recover well, and progress consistently.
