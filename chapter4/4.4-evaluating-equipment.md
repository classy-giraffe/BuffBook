## 4.4 Evaluating Machines, Free Weights, and Cables

The choice between free weights, cables, and machines is not one of ideology but of the resistance profile each tool produces and how well it satisfies the principles in 4.1. The right tool is the one that lets you safely and consistently expose the target muscle to high mechanical tension at long muscle lengths.
