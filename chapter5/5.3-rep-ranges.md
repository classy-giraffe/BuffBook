## 5.3 Rep Ranges: The Practical Continuum

Hypertrophy can be achieved across a broad spectrum of loading. Whether you perform 5 reps or 30 reps, the muscle can grow—provided the set is taken close enough to failure to recruit the necessary motor units. However, while all rep ranges are theoretically capable of building muscle, they are not all created equal in the gym.
