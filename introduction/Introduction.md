# State of the Industry
As of 2026, the fitness industry continues to recycle old solutions to the same problems. Many people show up to the gym with dedication, but lack a clear direction. Without a structured plan or an understanding of how to use equipment, they drift through random exercises, some ineffective, some outright unsafe. Thus the usual cycle starts: 
1. **Initial Enthusiasm:** The person starts with high motivation, often following a popular workout program or advice from social media.
2. **Lack of Progress:** After a few weeks or months, they hit a plateau. The initial gains from novelty wear off (if any), and they struggle to make further progress.
3. **Frustration and Confusion:** They become frustrated, often blaming themselves for not "working hard enough" or "not being genetically gifted." They may also encounter conflicting advice online, which adds to their confusion.
4. **Giving Up or Starting Over:** Many people either give up on their fitness goals or start over with a new program, hoping for better results, but often end up in the same cycle again.
