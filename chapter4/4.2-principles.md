## 4.2 Principles of Exercise Selection

A “good” hypertrophy exercise is not defined by how heavy it is or how much it burns. It is defined by whether it lets you deliver high mechanical tension to the target muscle, safely and sustainably, across the range of motion you intend to train. Five principles dominate this decision.
