1. Introduction: The Efficiency Mindset

This chapter introduces the core philosophy behind BuffBook: achieving maximum muscle growth with the minimum effective dose of training.

1.1 Who This Book Is For (The Minimalist Lifter) and Is Not For

This book is a collection of ideas, science, and hard-earned experience from my time under the bar. Too often, the fitness space overcomplicates training, turning simple principles into complex puzzles. Here, we demystify the process. We will explore how growth actually happens, establish reliable volume landmarks, and guide you through creating an effective program—from the variables at play to the nuances of exercise selection.
